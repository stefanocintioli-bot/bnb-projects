## What is it?

The **BNB Chain LatAm Learning Path** is a free, bilingual (Spanish/English) web-based learning platform — think Duolingo, but for blockchain and Web3 education. It lives at a single URL, requires no app download, no account creation, and works on any phone or laptop.

The goal is simple: **take a LatAm user with zero crypto knowledge and walk them through everything they need to understand and use BNB Chain**, in their language, at their pace.

---

## Who is it for?

- **Primary:** LatAm retail users — first-time crypto learners in Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Venezuela
- **Secondary:** Community managers, ambassadors, and partners who need an easy onboarding link to share
- **Not for:** Developers or traders (there are other resources for them)

## How Progress is Tracked

- **localStorage** — all progress is stored in the user's browser. No account, no backend.
- Each lesson shows a checkmark when completed.
- Each track has a live progress bar (0% → 33% → 66% → 100%).
- The homepage shows total progress: "X / 9 lessons completed."
- **Completion certificate** appears when all 9 lessons are done, with a share button.

No data is sent to any server. The user owns their own progress.

## Technical Details

- Single HTML file — zero dependencies, zero backend, zero maintenance costs
- Works offline after first load
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Dark theme with BNB yellow (#F0B90B)
- Fonts: Syne (headings) + IBM Plex Mono (code/labels) from Google Fonts
- Deployed on Vercel (free tier, global CDN, HTTPS)

LatAm is one of the fastest-growing crypto regions in the world — but most of the educational content is in English, technical, and aimed at people who already understand the basics. This platform fills that gap directly.

Every lesson completed = one more person in LatAm who understands blockchain, knows about BNB Chain, and is one step closer to using it. Every share = organic community growth.

This is education as growth marketing — and it costs nothing to maintain.
